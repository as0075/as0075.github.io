Privacy Policy for SecureDelete

Effective date: June 21, 2026

SecureDelete (“the app”, “we”, “us”, or “our”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles information when you use it.

Information We Collect

SecureDelete does not require an account or login. We do not collect personal user data.

Files and Device Data

SecureDelete only accesses files explicitly selected by you through Android’s system file picker. Selected file information is used only to display the selection and perform the deletion operation. The app does not upload selected files to our servers.

Advertising

This app may display advertisements through Google AdMob. Google and its advertising partners may process information such as device identifiers, IP address, approximate location inferred from IP address, app interactions, diagnostics, and advertising performance data to provide, measure, and personalize advertisements where permitted by law and your consent choices.

Where required, the app requests advertising consent through Google’s User Messaging Platform. You may review or change available privacy choices from the app’s settings or information screen.

Learn more about Google’s privacy practices: Google Privacy Policy.

Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. Information processed for advertising may be handled by Google AdMob and its advertising technology partners according to their policies and your consent choices.

Data Retention

SecureDelete does not store user data. Any information processed is used only during the current app session.

Security

We use reasonable technical measures appropriate to the app’s functionality. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure.

Children’s Privacy

SecureDelete is not intended for children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes to the app, services, or applicable requirements. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

Contact Us
